Mini Breakfast Burrito

‚è±Ô∏è Prep Time 2 min
üç≥ Cook Time 3 min
üë• Servings 1
ü•ö Difficulty Easy

A fast DIY breakfast perfect for busy mornings! This microwaveable burrito is quick to make and easy to eat on the go. With just a few simple ingredients, you'll have a satisfying breakfast in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1 slice reduced-fat American cheese
  • 1 flour tortilla
  • Nonstick cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Spray a microwave-safe cereal bowl with nonstick cooking spray. Crack the eggs into the bowl, add the salsa, and stir well to combine.
  2. Microwave on high for 1 minute, stir, and cook for another minute or until the egg mixture firms up and is fully cooked.
  3. Place the cheese slice in the center of the tortilla and top it with the hot egg mixture. Wrap it all up like a burrito, folding in the sides first, then rolling tightly. Enjoy immediately or wrap in foil for the road!
