Fire up succulent blackened salmon with an exciting blend of Cajun-style spices! This restaurant-quality dish is crispy on the outside, tender and flaky on the inside. Perfect for a healthy and flavorful dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons ground paprika
  • 1 tablespoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground white pepper
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Â¼ teaspoon dried thyme
  • Â¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • Â¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 salmon fillets, skin and bones removed
  • Â½ cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Mix paprika, cayenne pepper, onion powder, salt, white and black pepper, thyme, basil, and oregano together in a small bowl to create your Cajun spice blend.
  2. Brush salmon fillets on both sides with 1/4 cup melted butter, and sprinkle evenly with the spice mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons melted butter in a large, heavy skillet over high heat until very hot. Add salmon and cook until blackened, 2 to 5 minutes. Lift fillets, add remaining melted butter to the skillet, and flip fillets into the butter. Cook until the other side is blackened and fish flakes easily with a fork, 3 to 5 minutes. Serve immediately.
